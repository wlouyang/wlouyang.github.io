Biography

Wanli Ouyang obtained Ph.D from the Dept. of Electronic Engineering , the Chinese University of Hong Kong. He is now an associate professor at the University of Sydney. He was awarded Australian Research Council Future Fellowship, meaning that he will be exempted teaching and can focus on research in the next four years. His research interests include deep learning and its application to computer vision and pattern recognition, image and video processing.
